Guiding Lights

In this chaotic world,

We all yearn for those small, precious boosts, don't we?

That life-saving hug from a sister when everything seems to crumble,

That unwavering assurance from mama when uncertainties rumble,

That empowering confidence from dada when you're facing your darkest hour,

That heartfelt conversation with a friend when you're nearing your final power,

It's these moments that keep our spirits strong,

They might not change the storm,

But the strength they infuse in us makes the impossible seem possible.
